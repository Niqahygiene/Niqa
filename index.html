How Often Should Grease Traps Be Cleaned and Why Is Regular Cleaning Important?

Grease traps should be cleaned every week to 4 weeks, depending on usage, to prevent blockages and ensure smooth operation. Regular cleaning is essential to avoid costly repairs, maintain proper drainage, and comply with health and safety regulations.

What is sump pit cleaning, and why is it important?

Sump pit cleaning removes debris and sludge buildup, preventing blockages, flooding, and potential damage to your property.

How can drainage blockages be effectively removed?

Drainage blockages can be cleared using professional methods like jetting or mechanical tools to restore water flow and prevent future clogs.
